About Me

Hello! I'm Andrew, WM4G, an Extra class amateur radio operator based on the West Coast of Florida near Tampa. I've been licensed since November 2024. I have been enjoying portable operations, learning at my local club K4EX, and getting more involved in the ham radio world. For now, this is a fairly basic page to share a bit about my ham radio activities and interests. I hope to expand it over time as I get more involved and learn new things in this great hobby!

Operating Interests

  • POTA hunting — with occasional activations when I can get out to the parks
  • HF digital modes (FT8 for now)
  • Club activities like Field Day, Florida Parks on The Air, Winter Field Day, special event stations, and public service events
  • VHF/UHF Local Nets

Looking forward to doing more park activations in the future. There's something special about operating portable in nature.

Portable Radio Setup

  • Yaesu FT-891 transceiver
  • LDG auto tuner
  • POTA Explorer 20' carbon fiber mast
  • FlatlandersMirrors 67ft 40m, 20m, 15m, 10m EFHW antenna

Used for portable activations and POTA hunting at my QTH.

Volunteer Examiner

I'm a VE with both ARRL and Laurel VEC as part of EPARS/K4EX VE Team.

If you're in the area and looking to test, reach out to the EPARS/K4EX VE Team or look for a upcoming session on our Hamstudy.org page.

Club Membership

Member of the East Pasco Amateur Radio Society (K4EX).
